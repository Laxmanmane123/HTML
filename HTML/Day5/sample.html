In appearance, he is white in colour with brown bright eyes. It also has long fluffy hair on its body and a bushy tail which is loved by everyone in our home. I give him bread and milk every day. He is especially fond of meat and biscuits. Baba is a very faithful animal and protects our home. Whenever he sees an unknown person entering our house, he barks loudly at the strangers. He is a very charming and child-friendly animal. He is loyal and active as well. He sleeps early at night and wakes up late in the morning. When I come back from school, he jumps into my lap. In the evening, we go to the park for a walk and sometimes even play together with balls and flying discs. I am very happy with my dog, Baba. He has become an important member of our home.
